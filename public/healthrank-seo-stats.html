Clinical Impact

Why This Matters for Your Clinic
Translating the data into practice-level decisions
01
Your Patients Are Already Searching
With 77% of patients using Google before booking and 100 billion health searches yearly, the question isn't whether patients will search — it's whether they'll find you or your competitor.
02
Page 2 Might as Well Not Exist
91.5% of users never scroll past page one. A clinic ranked #11 receives less than 1% of clicks — effectively invisible to potential patients searching for care right now.
03
Local SEO Is Your Highest-ROI Channel
Local SEO converts at ~50% versus just 3% for social media ads. SEO leads close at 14.6% vs 1.7% for outbound. No other channel comes close to this return.
04
Mobile Optimization Is Non-Negotiable
60% of healthcare searches happen on smartphones. 44% of patients who find a provider via mobile search go on to book. A slow or un-optimized mobile site bleeds patients daily.
05
Reviews Are the New Referrals
84% of patients check online reviews before booking, and 61% trust reviews more than personal referrals. Reputation management is now core to patient acquisition — not optional.
06
A Poor Website Drives Patients Away
71% of patients will leave to find another provider if your website lacks sufficient information. Your website is your most critical intake form — before a patient ever calls.
